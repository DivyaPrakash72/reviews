In India we need more movies like Animal.

Certainly! “Animal”, a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action drama film, directed and co-written by Sandeep Reddy Vanga, is a gripping tale of vengeance and destruction.

The film stars Ranbir Kapoor as Ranvijay Singh, a violent man who discovers an assassination plot against his estranged father. Fueled by love and rage, he embarks on a path of retribution. The movie draws parallels to “The Godfather”, with its portrayal of family dynamics, power struggles, and toxic masculinity. Despite mixed reviews, “Animal” shattered box office records, grossing over ₹917.82 crore worldwide.
